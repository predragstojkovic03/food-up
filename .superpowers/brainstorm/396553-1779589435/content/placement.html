A — Inside the preview dialog

Extra quantities appear in the send dialog, below the intro/subject fields. Entered right before sending. Not persisted separately — they're baked into the email and stored with it.

Pros

  • One place to review everything before sending
  • Simpler data model

Cons

  • Must re-enter on every resend
  • Not reflected in XLSX export

B — In the window detail panel (before send)

A persistent "Extra quantities" section lives in the expanded window panel, alongside the XLSX download and supplier table. Saved to the DB when entered. Automatically included in all subsequent sends and exports.

Pros

  • Persisted — survives resends
  • Can factor into XLSX and cost totals
  • Clear separation from email editing

Cons

  • Manager must remember to fill before sending
  • Slightly more complex data model
